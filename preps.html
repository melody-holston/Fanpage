Preps

Preps are important to master before trying and extended.

    Two leg prep

  • Top Girl, stand up straight out of your load keeping hips aligned and under shoulders.
  • Bases, out of load plant your feet and use your legs to dip and drive through to chest level, making sure to rotate wrist while keeping great coverage of foot.
  • Backspot, from load keep top girls ankles together, dipping with your legs and driving that to girl through to chest level height.

    Single leg prep

  • Top Girl, load will start with right foot in the load the other foot is touching the floor is up on your toe, hands on shoulders
  • Standing up quickly on top of your right leg, connecting left foot to right knee while squeezing core.
  • Bases, out of load plant your feet and use your legs to dip and drive through to chest level, making sure to rotate wrist while keeping great coverage of foot.
  • Backspot, from load keep top girls ankles together, dipping with your legs and driving that to girl through to chest level height.
